Enrollment Process

Enrolling at Tiny Titans Tech Academy is simple. Our coordinators guide you through our pathways to ensure optimal placement.

  • 1

    Submit Query Form

    Fill in student particulars, age, school/college details, and chosen technical program.

  • 2

    Counselor Call

    Our program coordinators will reach out within 24 hours to schedule a free demo and align batch slots.

  • 3

    Batch Allocation & Kit Handover

    Confirm batch timings (weekday/weekend) and collect student kits/textbooks at our Veppampattu center.
